# 01.1 — Customer Personas

## Primary personas

### Persona 1: The Café Owner / Manager ("Maria")
- Runs a 2-4 location indie café or one coffee shop with steady volume
- Buying chai mix, frappé bases, cocoa, syrups for the menu
- Orders every 4-8 weeks, $200-800 per order
- Loyal to specific SKUs (Big Train Vanilla Chai, David Rio Tiger Spice, etc.) — reorders the same thing
- **What she needs:** fast reorder, clear pricing, reliable delivery, legitimacy signals (she's a business, not playing)
- **What she's NOT:** a browser — she opens the site, finds the SKU, orders, leaves

### Persona 2: The Office Manager ("Jordan")
- Stocks the office kitchen
- Buys hot cocoa, oatmeal cups, nicer coffee supplies
- Orders quarterly-ish, $150-400 per order
- Less brand-loyal, more swayed by "what's new" and employee requests
- **What they need:** easy browsing, variety packs, business billing info fields
- **What they're NOT:** price-obsessed — convenience matters more

### Persona 3: The Home Enthusiast ("Dave")
- Loves his chai. Bought a bag at a farmer's market 8 years ago, has been reordering from La Costa Gourmet ever since
- Orders every 2-3 months, $80-150 per order (usually right around the free-shipping threshold)
- Has tunnel vision — buys the same 2-3 products every time
- **What he needs:** past-order reorder, "you also might like" (to break tunnel vision, which grandpa explicitly called out)
- **What he's NOT:** interested in B2B content — he skips it

### Persona 4: The Bulk Gift Buyer ("Helen")
- Buys variety packs or brand samplers as gifts for family
- Orders 2-4 times a year, around holidays
- Needs clear product descriptions, pretty photos, gift-ready variety
- **What she needs:** photography that makes products look appetizing, clear "what's in this pack"
- **What she's NOT:** a repeat customer for the same SKU

## Secondary / edge cases

- Cafés in **Hawaii or Alaska** — rare but real, they pay the $25 surcharge
- **Bulk / wholesale** inquiries — v1 has a "For Business" page with contact form and downloadable line sheets; no real wholesale portal
- International — **not supported in v1**

## What this means for the design

- **Logged-in home page must surface reorders from past** (Persona 1 and 3) — not just featured products
- **"You might also like"** is a real feature, not a nice-to-have — grandpa specifically called out tunnel vision (Persona 3)
- **Product detail pages need credible B2B signals** — pack size prominent, volume discount messaging ("Save on 6+ cases — contact us"), clear spec table
- **Homepage has to serve both** — can't be cutesy enough to alienate Persona 1 or stiff enough to lose Persona 4
- **Category browsing matters for Personas 2 and 4**, but Personas 1 and 3 bypass it entirely via search + reorder

## Who the site is NOT for

- Not for people looking for Whole Foods-tier organic branding (wrong category)
- Not for Amazon-speed same-day shipping expectations (dropship reality = 3-7 days)
- Not for international buyers (US-only shipping v1)
- Not for people price-comparing against Walmart (this is specialty, not commodity)
